About PMSSS

The Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS) is an initiative to support students from across India in pursuing their higher education goals. The scheme provides financial assistance to deserving students through a streamlined, paperless scholarship disbursement process.

Step 1: Register

Create an account on the portal to start your scholarship application.

Step 2: Submit Documents

Upload the required documents securely through our online portal.

Step 3: Get Verified

Your documents will be verified by the SAG Bureau for eligibility.

Step 4: Receive Scholarship

Upon approval, the scholarship funds will be disbursed directly to your bank account.
